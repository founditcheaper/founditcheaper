How it works
Every day at the time you set above, your Top Deal Picks are automatically replaced with a fresh, randomized set of 10 deals from your catalog — a mix of Amazon & Walmart that favors promo-code deals and big brands.
You can hand-edit the picks anytime after the refresh — promote from All Deals, remove, reorder, or randomize. Your changes stay live until the next morning's run.
