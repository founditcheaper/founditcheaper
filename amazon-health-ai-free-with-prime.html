prime perk

amazon built a health ai, and
prime members get free doctor chats

there is a health assistant in the amazon app now. it answers health questions, and if you have prime it comes with a handful of free messages to a real doctor.

posted july 22, 2026 · rolling out to us customers

the catch: the ai chatbot is free for everyone, but it is a helper, not a doctor. amazon's own words: it is not intended for diagnosis or treatment without a care provider. the actually valuable part, the free visits with a person, is a prime perk, and it caps at five.

what prime actually gets you: amazon says eligible us prime members get up to five free direct-message consultations with a One Medical provider, for more than 30 common conditions, a value of up to $145. no prime, the ai is still free, but a live telehealth visit runs $29.

what it does

straight from amazon's description, not our guess.
1

answers health questions, 24/7

you type a question in the amazon app or on amazon.com and it answers in plain language. it runs any hour.

2

can read your records, only if you let it

it can look at your history, medications and lab results to give a more personal answer, but only after you give One Medical permission. that part is optional. skip it and it still answers general questions.

3

hands you to a real person when it matters

it can book appointments, help with prescription renewals, and connect you to a One Medical provider by message or video. prime is where the first few of those messages are free.

read Amazon's announcement →
plain talk: this is a convenience tool, not a replacement for your doctor, and Amazon says as much. it is handy for the small stuff and for not sitting on hold. for anything real, it points you to a person, which is the right call. if something is urgent, call your doctor or 911, not a chatbot.

the fine print

so you know what you are actually signing up for.
  • the Health AI assistant itself is free and lives in the amazon app and on amazon.com. rolling out to us customers now.
  • prime members get up to five free message consultations with a One Medical provider for 30-plus common conditions, valued up to $145.
  • without prime the ai is still free, but a live pay-per-visit telehealth visit is $29.
  • it only reads your medical records if you grant One Medical permission. that is opt-in, not automatic.
  • amazon states it is not intended for diagnosis or treatment without a care provider, and it routes you to a human when it is unsure.
  • us only for now.
  • source: Amazon's own announcement. terms and pricing are Amazon's and can change, so check the app.
