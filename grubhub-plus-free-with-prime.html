prime perk

grubhub+ is free if you
already pay for prime

unlimited $0 delivery fees on eligible orders. most people paying for prime have this sitting unused, because you have to switch it on yourself.

posted july 22, 2026 · ongoing prime benefit

the catch: it is free delivery, not free food. you still pay for the food, tax and tip, and the $0 delivery only applies once the order clears grubhub's subtotal minimum. service fees get reduced, not erased. and it lasts exactly as long as you keep paying for prime.

the part that is actually clean: it does not roll into a paid membership. amazon's page says you will not be charged for grubhub+, and if you drop prime the grubhub+ side just stops. no trial clock, no card charge waiting at the end of it.

how to turn it on

one link-up, then it stays on.
1

go to amazon's grubhub page while signed in

sign in to the amazon account that has prime on it, then open the activation page.

activate grubhub+ on amazon →
2

hit activate and link your grubhub account

you connect amazon and grubhub once. new to grubhub, you make an account in the same step.

3

grab the first-order credit while you are in there

amazon is currently showing $5 off your first $15 order with code FIRSTORDER at checkout. codes rotate, so take it when you see it.

the fine print

so nobody is surprised at checkout.
  • free for as long as your prime membership is active. cancel prime and grubhub+ ends with it.
  • it does not auto-renew into a paid grubhub+ membership. amazon states you will not be charged.
  • $0 delivery applies to eligible orders that meet grubhub's subtotal minimum. taxes, tip and other fees still apply.
  • service fees are reduced on eligible orders, not removed.
  • it also includes 5% back in grubhub+ credit on eligible pickup orders.
  • the $5 off $15 offer is for a first order and uses code FIRSTORDER. promo codes change, so check the page.
  • source: Amazon's own Grubhub+ with Prime help page. terms are Amazon's and Grubhub's, so check them before you order.
