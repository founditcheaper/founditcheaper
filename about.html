About & FAQ

founditcheaper.net
What founditcheaper is

founditcheaper is a deals site for people who do not want to be sold to. We pull real discounts from Amazon and Walmart, put them in one place, and update them every day. No hype words, no fake countdowns, no "you won't believe this." Just the item, the price, and a link.

The audience is simple: working people who like a good deal and do not have time to dig through a hundred coupon sites to find one. If a deal is not actually a good price, it does not belong here.

How we find deals

Deals come in through the retailers' own official product data, not by scraping. Every deal is checked before it goes up. We rank by real discount, product rating, and review count, and we filter out inflated "discounts" where a seller quietly raised the list price to fake a bigger markdown.

  • Sourced through official retailer programs (Amazon and Walmart)
  • Prices shown are pulled live, not stored, so they stay current
  • Suspiciously large markdowns on no-name items are hidden automatically
  • Promo-code deals are labeled, with the code shown on the deal
Who runs this

founditcheaper.net is a small business based in the Rio Grande Valley of South Texas. It started as a way to share the deals we were already hunting for ourselves and grew into a daily site plus a large following across social media and email.

You can reach a real person at deals@founditcheaper.net.

Frequently asked questions
Are these deals real?
Yes. Deals come from the retailers' official data and are checked before posting. That said, prices and promo codes change fast, so always confirm the final price at checkout before you buy.
How often is the site updated?
Every day. New deals come in throughout the day, and the top of the list rotates so you are not looking at the same products every time you visit.
Does founditcheaper make money, and does it cost me anything?
founditcheaper earns a small commission when you buy through our links, including as an Amazon Associate. It costs you nothing extra, and it is what keeps the site free. We only post deals we think are actually worth it.
How do the promo codes work?
Promo codes are provided by sellers and entered at checkout. They run on the seller's budget, so a code can stop working once that budget runs out, even if it worked earlier in the day. Codes are shown right on the deal, written like "code SAVE20."
Do I need an account?
No. You can browse, search, and filter every deal without signing up for anything.
Can I get told when a specific item goes on sale?
Yes. Use the Deal Alert page to tell us the item you are watching, and we will email you if a matching deal shows up. You can also join the daily email for a short roundup of the best deals.
How do you decide what goes at the top?
The top is ranked by real value, meaning the size of the discount, the product's rating, and how many reviews it has, with a filter that removes fake or inflated discounts. The lineup rotates daily so the same products do not sit at the top every day.
Which stores do you cover?
Amazon and Walmart today, with more retailers planned. Each deal shows which store it is from.
I am a seller. Can I get my deal featured?
Yes. Use the Submit a Deal page to send it in. Submissions are reviewed before they go live.
